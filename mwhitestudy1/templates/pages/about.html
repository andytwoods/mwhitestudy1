About the Study

MWhiteStudy1 is a research platform designed for behavioral and HCI-style studies.

Our Methodology

We use a staged flow approach where participants are guided through a sequence of screens. Each screen can present content, such as text and images, followed by questions to capture judgments, decisions, and comprehension.

Technology Stack

  • Django: Robust backend for data management and security.
  • HTMX: Seamless, interactive user experience without full page reloads.
  • Bootstrap: Responsive design for accessibility across devices.

Data Privacy

All data collected is stored securely and used solely for research purposes. We prioritize participant anonymity and data integrity.
